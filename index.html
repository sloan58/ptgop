I. The Committee

The Peters Township Republican Committee is composed of twenty-four locally-elected Precinct Committee People, one man and one woman serving each of the township’s twelve precincts. Elected to four-year terms, they are your neighbors, your advocates, and your direct line to the Republican Party at every level of government.
